A location sharing mobile application that uses smart systems to help people manage safety and risk at locations.

Core Lite is compromised of three modules. Locations (Location management), Help and Guidance, design to help you Prevent, Detect and Respond.

Locations

  • Enables you to create a safety plan for a location
  • Share your location automatically with contacts on check in/out
  • Prompts you to adjust your plan on early arrival at a location
  • Prompts if you early leave a location and shares this with selected contacts
  • At the end of your plan timer, if you have not exited the location, extended or completed the plan, a step escalation process starts, with a help notification being issued six minutes after the end of the plan
  • Safety plan history is not viewable, in the event your phone is viewed by others

Help

  • On activation, the information you need to accelerate the help process is SMS shared with Contacts you selected as part of your safety plan:
    • a short narrative detailing help activation
    • your name and phone number
    • your live location on an online map
    • your phone battery percentage
    • your altitude
    • how many Contacts nominated to Help
  • Your live location is shared until you choose to cancel Help.
  • Keep your Contacts in the loop with a unique one touch button

Guidance

Access category specific, summary information that you can use to help you respond to the unplanned.

The safety area is an adjustable area around a location. It sets the area where safety monitoring applies. i.e. if you don’t leave the area at the end of the safety plan the help process is activated.
Adjust the area that applies to the environment.
By default the area is 30ft (10m).
In metro locations, keep a smaller safety area, so if needed, your selected Contacts can find you. In country areas, assign a larger area, to minimise notifications and unnecessary SMS sharing.

As you approach the location listed in the safety plan, a red circle is displayed around the location. This lets you know you have not arrived at the location and the safety plan is not active.
Once a user arrives at a location, the circle around the location will turn green, so you know user you are checked in.
If selected in the safety plan, SMS notifications are automatically sent out.

This indicates that you are checked in at a location and your safety plan is active.

If you arrive at a location earlier than the scheduled safety plan time, you will be asked whether you want to commence the plan now. If not, the plan activates when scheduled.

If you arrive at a location after the location plan start time, you will receive a notification asking whether they want to start the plan or adjust safety plan timer. Aegis 24/7 will not start without this.
If you have not arrived at a location within two hours of the scheduled start time, the plan will auto delete.

Active tracking is needed to tell the app when to start the safety plan. Tracking occurs from the point where you created the safety plan, to the intended location. Tracking stops when this occurs. Your data is not shared.

Once a safety is active, a summary panel is displayed on the Home screen.

This lets you at a glance the status of your safety plan.

There are three sections to the panel:

  • The status of the safety plan.
  • The duration of the plan, including start and end.
  • Quick 10. Add 10 minutes to the end of a safety plan. Select for multiple 10 minute adds.

At the end of the scheduled time, a user receives an on screen text notification, audible alert and vibration at the end of the plan

You can choose to:

  • Leave the location
  • Edit the plan and extend the time
  • Quick add 10 minutes
  • Complete the plan

If you make no selection, the help escalation commences, as it assumes you are unable to respond.

You have six minutes to respond before Help SMS are sent out.

Process overview:

  • Timer ends – 1st notification issued.
  • Two minute grace period.
  • You are still at the location and within the safety area – 2nd notification issued.
  • Two minute grace period.
  • You are still at the location and within the safety area – 3rd notification issued.
  • Two minute grace period.
  • Help SMS issued to selected Contacts

Help can be activated one of two ways:

  • Manually
    No safety plan has been created. For when you need help and have not created a safety plan.
  • Automatically
    A safety plan has been created, you are at the location and have not responded to the escalation process to verify that you are okay. After six minutes Help SMS are sent out.

The stepped automated escalation process is used to:

  • Activate Help if you have become injured or unconscious and unable to activate the Help button
  • Avoid unintended Help SMS alerts due to a misplaced phone or similar.
